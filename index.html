A To Do List That Helps You Forget

Traditional To Do lists start out strong but eventually become burdens. When capturing tasks outpaces our ability to complete them, we find ourselves in a death grip with our own lists, constantly maintaining them instead of focusing on what truly matters.

TOS is a proof of concept inspired by writing in sand on a beach. Like sand that holds writing well but gradually fades away with the tides, your tasks will fade each day until they naturally disappear. Each task has a lifespan of 5 days but can be renewed similar to retracing the faded writing in the sand. This pattern encourages focusing on what truly matters while remaining flexible to changing priorities.

Simply drag tasks above others to renew them, or let them fade away naturally releasing the shackles to arbitrary priorities from the past.

Note: This is a POC, but your data is fully persisted to your account and the app is fully functional. Tasks are stored and synchronized across devices when you sign in.
