What is this about?

Traditional to-do lists often become overwhelming maintenance burdens that drain productivity instead of enhancing it. When capturing tasks outpaces our ability to complete them, we find ourselves in a death grip with our own lists, constantly maintaining them instead of focusing on what truly matters.

This app explores a different approach inspired by writing in sand on a beach. Like sand that holds writing well but gradually fades away with the tides, your tasks will fade each day until they naturally disappear after 5 days unless you actively renew them. This encourages you to focus on what's truly important while remaining flexible to changing priorities.
